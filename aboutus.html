About Us

We introduce ourselves as one of the leading transport Company of India founded in January, 1985 having their own fleet of 475 trucks, including Car Carrier Trailers/Goods Containers Trailers / Multi XL Containers. Having an experience of 25 years and a turnover of Rs.140 Crores, we have establishments all over India.

Mahaveera Transport (P) Limited are especially specialized in our car carrier services, with a fleet of Car Carriers reaching 200 and catering to the needs of all major car manufacturers in the country.

We render our services to well known National and Multi National Companies in Public as well as in Private Sectors. We are approved by INDIAN BANKS ASSOCIATION, under code DLM-1011.

We have been awarded with many prestigious awards including Bharat Udyog and Ratna Udyog, for our uncompromised service and contribution to the value chains of several important products.

History

Mahaveera Transport (P) Limited was came in to existence in 1985. In the title of Mahaveera Transport Corporation amongst the 1st transporters for the carriage of Maruti Cars from Maruti Udyog Limited-Gurgaon Haryana.

Mr.S.K.Jain has the distinction of his invention “Wheels on Wheels” concept. He designed and fabricated the Double Decker Vehicles to carry the cars manufactured by Maruti Udyog to keep the cars in perfect shape, as manufactured in the company. The concept was appreciated by one and all and is in continuance till date.

With him, his brothers, Mr. R. K. Jain, A. K. Jain and Sanjay Jain taken positions at Delhi, Bangalore and Mumbai as other directors of the company and are controlling the activities of the enterprise.

Incidentally, this is the silver jubilee year of the company, and Maruti Udyog Limited has awarded us with Life Time Achievement Award for the un-compromised services being rendered to them.
