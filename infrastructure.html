Infrastructure

Over the years, the company has built up the infrastructure and management to provide a customer friendly organization to meet your logistic requirements with optimal efficiency.

Infrastructure & IT

The company has its fully computerized zonal offices, which provides requisite interface of our branches with the corporate headquarters, so that can trace the consignments immediately as well to provide delivery on time.

We have developed our own ERP, taking into account the customized requirements of all our esteemed customers.
We are in the process of installing GPS facilities on all our trucks. This would enable us to track their progress efficiently. This will also reduce our transport time further and enhance your experience. We are also studying the feasibility of the RFID technology.

We have a huge network of associate companies and partners due to our experience and relationship within the industry, whose vital support enables us to offer whatever you want, whenever you want.

Private Workshops

The organization had developed in house facilities for repairing and maintenance of its fleet, and has proven experience in designing and fabrication of specialized truck carriers currently in service with companies who are national leaders in their field. We have our own workshops where we repair and maintain our fleet, especially due to our ever-increasing capacity.

New inductions to the fleet are also inducted regularly, as and when the need of customers arises. The new inductions include specialized automobile self-loading carriers and hybrids to service the white and goods industry. What this means quite simply is that we are not only CARGO carriers – we design build, maintain and run them.

Experienced Workforce

We have committed translating reliable and efficient logistic solutions into reality. This involves superior carriers an experienced and dedicated workforce and improved management aided by information system.

We have experienced staff and our drivers have immense experience of driving in extreme conditions. We are also considering a proposal of opening a Driver Coaching School that will be a complete training center.
